What & Why

Typical references to secondary sites, images, and other forms of data stored on the server is accessed with the a tag. This is the hyperlink tag. Hyperlinks can be used for placing a link in text, photos, or even div sections. The following illustrates three examples:

Page Link
Image Link
DIV Link

In all three examples there is a pattern. The object that holds the link to the needed information is always wrapped around by the hyperlink. The a tag then can be modified to specify specific behavior supported by HTML. This is important to understand in the next section as the download attribute builds on it.
